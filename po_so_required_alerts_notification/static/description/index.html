SALES - PURCHASE ALERT NOTIFICATION

Overview

It is used to give an alert notification if the user fails to enter the required information in the sales and the purchase form.

Key Features

Sales and Purchase Orderliness Alert Notification.

Sales and Purchase Unit Price Alert Notification.

Sales and Purchase Order Quantity Alert Notification.
