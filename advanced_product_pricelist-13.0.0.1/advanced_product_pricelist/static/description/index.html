Advanced Product Pricelist

Print from multiple menus

You can print pricelists from products, products variants, partners, and pricelists form and list view. In order to fully enable this module, you need to activate "Advanced price rules" at Settings -> Sales -> Pricing -> Pricelists.

Customizable pricelist report

It's possible to select to print pricelist with or without product images and to select the desired size. You can choose between small, medium and big. You also can print product description and show cost price, pricelist price, and sale price.
